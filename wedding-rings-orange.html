Choosing the Right Metal for Your Wedding Ring

Each metal offers a unique blend of durability, appearance, and feel, helping you find the perfect match for your lifestyle and style.

Platinum

  • A premium choice known for its exceptional durability and naturally white luster. Platinum resists tarnish and wear, making it ideal for a lifetime of daily wear with minimal maintenance.

Gold

  • A timeless favorite available in yellow, white, and rose tones.
    — 18K Gold offers a luxurious balance of purity and strength
    — 14K Gold is slightly less pure but more durable, making it a practical choice for everyday wear.

Palladium

  • A lightweight, hypoallergenic alternative to platinum, palladium offers a bright, contemporary look with excellent resistance to tarnish and wear—ideal for those with sensitive skin.

Tungsten Carbide

  • Perfect for active lifestyles, tungsten carbide is highly scratch-resistant and incredibly durable. Its weight and strength make it especially popular for men’s wedding bands.
