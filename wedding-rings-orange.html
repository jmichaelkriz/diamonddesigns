Choosing the Right Metal for Your Wedding Ring

  • Platinum: Known for its exceptional durability and naturally white luster, platinum is a premium choice that resists tarnish and wear.
  • Gold: Available in yellow, white, and rose hues, gold remains a timeless favorite. 18K gold provides a luxurious balance of purity and strength, while 14K gold offers added durability for everyday wear.
  • Palladium: A lightweight, hypoallergenic alternative to platinum, palladium offers a sleek, contemporary look.
  • Tungsten Carbide: Ideal for those with active lifestyles, this metal is scratch-resistant and durable, making it perfect for men’s wedding bands.
